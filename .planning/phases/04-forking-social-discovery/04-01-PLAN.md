# Phase 04-01: Database Models for Forking and Follow System

## Objective

Create database models for fork lineage tracking (parent_id, root_id), soft delete with archive (is_archived), dataset forking, follow relationships, and event tracking for future ML. This provides the foundation for all Phase 4 social features.

Purpose: Enable fork attribution chains, prevent deletion of notebooks with forks, support follow-based social graph, and collect engagement data for trending algorithm.

Output: Updated Notebook and Dataset models with fork lineage, new Follow and FeedEvent models, database migration, and Pydantic schemas.
